Perfume Care Tips

  • Store perfumes in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Apply perfume to pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears) for longer-lasting scent.
  • Do not rub your wrists together after applying perfume—it can break down the fragrance.
  • Keep bottles tightly closed to prevent evaporation and preserve quality.
